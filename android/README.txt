This creates an android distribution of infinispan-core project (with dependencies) packaged into one single jar.

Based on ideas from diploma thesis "Integrácia Infinispan funkcionality do platformy Android" by Bc. Jaroslav Jankovič

offers alternative porting method and packaging of infinispan dependencies
